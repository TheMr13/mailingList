About us

The group is for people looking to meet others in their 20s and 30s and have fun together. Please note that this is not a dating group though, we offer a safe environment to meet new people and make friends.

Whether you are new to Auckland, a busy working professional, or simply keen to try new activities it all boils down to the same thing â€“ finding a group of like minded people and finding it fast.

We run a huge variety of events on a regular basis including drinks, theatre, pool, yoga, salsa, tennis, rock climbing, brunch, food events, art exhibitions, sketching, bingo social nights, quiz nights and girls mingle events.
We're a not for profit group run entirely by voluntary organisers.
